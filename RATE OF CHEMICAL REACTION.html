RATE OF CHEMICAL REACTION
Rate of chemical reaction is the number of moles of rectants converted or product formed per unit time
factors affecting the rate of chemical reaction
  1. TEMPERATURE

    2. An increase in temperature lead to an increase in the rate of reaction while a decrease in temperature will lead to a decrease in the rate of reaction. This is because an increase in temperature at constant volume will to an inccreace in kinetic energy of the particles making the particles to move faster rapidly colliding with each other.The reaction goes faster because the energy release is greater than the activation energy barrier.
